This is an example about combining a ranking design with a timeseries design. The connection between the two is obtained by the animated transitions.

The categorical scale, which identifies the urban agglomerations, resides on the Y axis and the scale for the quantitative values (population) resides on the X axis. In other words, the position of the bars encode the categorical values while their length encode the quantitative values.

  • Use the controls above to navigate through time.
  • Click on the bars to highlight specific urban agglomerations.
